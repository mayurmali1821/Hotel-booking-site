Our Team

We pride ourselves in being one of the most eco-friendly hotels in the temple city of Udupi. Right from the ground up, the materials used for construction, The waste water from the sewage treatment plant (STP) is connected to the adjacent Public Park to water the Plants. From the latest 5 star amenities to the greenscape across the hotel, Samanvay Udupi has made important environmental improvements to its structure in order to minimize its impact on the environment. We simply believe in being a responsible lodging that follows the practices of green living. The vertical gardens add to the beauty of the hotel building. The building materials come from sustainable resources. The structure maximizes air flow to reduce the need for air conditioning. To strengthen our allegiance/dedication to eco responsibility, our staff shares our passionate commitment...
